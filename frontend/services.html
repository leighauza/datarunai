Three services. One goal: clear data.

Data services built for founders.

From cleaning your data to automating your processes, we handle the infrastructure so you can focus on growing.

01
Data Audit
Clean data for a healthy business

Messy data slows down good businesses. We audit your data infrastructure—across all sources—to identify gaps, inconsistencies, and corruption. By standardizing your data, we build the solid foundation needed for scalable, high-confidence decision-making.

What we deliver:

  • Comprehensive assessment of your data sources
  • Detailed findings and remediation plan
  • Data standardization and cleanup
  • Documentation of your new data structure
02
Data Lift
Key insights that drive better decisions.

You can't manage what you can't see. We build dashboards connected to your live business data—revenue, customer behavior, inventory movement, cash flow. Get daily updates on what's happening in your business. Spot patterns, catch problems early, and make decisions with confidence.

What we deliver:

  • Integrated data connections across your systems
  • Custom dashboards built around your KPIs
  • Daily automated updates
  • Team access and documentation
03
Data Flow
AI integrations that actually work.

Automations fail silently when your data architecture is wrong. We design how information moves between your systems—payments, inventory, accounting, and AI tools—to ensure everything stays in sync. Your processes run reliably, and your integrations work seamlessly.

What we deliver:

  • Data architecture design and implementation
  • System integrations and API connections
  • Monitoring, alerts, and error handling
  • Documentation and operational runbooks
