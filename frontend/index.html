How It Works

One chatbot. Trained on your business.
Running 24/7.

Every DataRunAI chatbot is built specifically for your restaurant or online store — your menu, your FAQs, your delivery areas, your tone. We set it up, we host it, we keep it updated.

The Process
From discovery call to live chatbot in about a month.
  • 🧠
    Step 1 — Discovery Call

    We map how your business actually runs — your most common customer questions, your menu or catalog, delivery areas, and how you speak to customers.

    We leave with a clear blueprint for your chatbot.
  • ⚙️
    Step 2 — We Build & Train

    We handle everything behind the scenes — building your chatbot from scratch, training it on your business data, and structuring flows for inquiries, orders, and leads.

    You simply review and approve before launch.
  • 🧪
    Step 3 — Test & Refine

    Before going live, we run it through real customer scenarios, refine the responses, and fix edge cases.

    This ensures smooth, reliable performance from day one.
  • 🚀
    Step 4 — Goes Live

    Your chatbot launches on Facebook Messenger, your website, or both — answering questions, capturing leads, and taking orders from the moment it's live.

  • 🔄
    Step 5 — We Manage Everything

    Your monthly retainer covers hosting, uptime monitoring, content updates as your menu or offerings change, and support whenever something needs fixing.

    A system that runs 24/7 without any extra effort from you.
